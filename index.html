Our Story

We are Clark and Rose.

We were just two simple people with different paths that crossed and intertwined on one fateful day.

We met on Move-In Day freshman year at National University. Clark and his friend were scoping out the girls in our hallway because they had been on campus for a while for basketball. I was standing with my good friend and roommate at the opposite end of the hall watching and laughing at them. When they made it to our end of the hall we introduced ourselves and decided to all hang out that night.

The rest is history!

Through ups and downs and now to walk the aisle and become one.
